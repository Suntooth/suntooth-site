homelang

but the river takes her shape from every tempest she abides
and like her, you'll be made new again.

homelang (not the final name) is my current conlang project. it's progressing in dribs and drabs, but i'm proud of what it is
so far. while fpapyle (unintentionally) had a theme of monarchy and related subjects in its vocabulary, homelang's
vocabulary is more focused on nature vs artificiality, to the point where the first temporary name i thought of was
"natlang" (which would be great irony :P ). hopefully i'll keep working on the language!
